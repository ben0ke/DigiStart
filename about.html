Our Mission

DigiStart is an innovative company specializing in spreading digital knowledge and teaching basic IT skills. Our goal is to help different age groups and communities acquire up-to-date digital knowledge and practical IT skills. Our services include organizing IT courses, workshops, and online trainings at different levels, where participants can learn the basics of computing, programming, and digital security. DigiStart is committed to making digital knowledge accessible to everyone and contributing to increasing digital literacy in our community. Our aim is to help as many people as possible enter the digital world confidently and safely. Join us and step into the digital future with the help of DigiStart!



Our Impact

DigiStart’s areas of impact are as follows:

  • Education and Training: Teaching basic IT skills at various levels, including beginner, intermediate, and advanced courses.
  • Organizing Workshops: Conducting interactive workshops where participants gain practical experience in using digital tools and software.
  • Online Courses: Providing online trainings on a digital platform, enabling flexible learning.
  • Curriculum Development: Developing modern and relevant curriculum that meets market demands and technological advancement.
  • Consultation and Advice: Providing personalized guidance for individual and corporate clients in developing digital skills.
  • Community Programs: Organizing community events and programs to promote digital literacy.
