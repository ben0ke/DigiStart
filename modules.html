Digital Knowledge Course

This course equips learners with foundational digital literacy, online tools, safety, and problem-solving skills to thrive in today's connected world.

Learning Objectives

By the end of this course, you will be able to:

  • ✓ Understand core concepts of digital literacy and online citizenship
  • ✓ Use productivity tools like email, word processors, and cloud storage
  • ✓ Practice safe internet use and manage digital identity
  • ✓ Recognize reliable digital information sources

Course Modules

Module 1: Introduction to Digital Literacy

What is digital knowledge, and why is it important?

Module 2: Productivity & Communication Tools

Learn to use essential software: email, documents, spreadsheets, video calls.

Module 3: Internet Safety & Security

Explore safe browsing, strong passwords, scams, and protecting personal data.

Module 4: Navigating Information Online

How to identify credible sources, fake news, and use search engines effectively.
